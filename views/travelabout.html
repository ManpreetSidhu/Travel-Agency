About Us


We are a Canadian based travel agency that specializes in finding the best deals available.
Weather you are looking for a cheep get-away for the weekend or a vacation to paradise we got you covered!






Our Story


We started as a small group of college students that wanted to take vacations. After hours of reasurce we soon realized that few travel agencies actually offered deals that would be equivalent to what you would get if you booked everything your self.
So that was the problem we decided to solve!
Our goal is to find the most value possible for your money.
